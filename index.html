things to do in Lucknow

1) Food

Awadhi cuisine is a cuisine native to the Awadh region in Northern India. The city is also known for its Nawabi foods.

2) Places

Best notable places to visit are the Bara Imambara, the Chota Imambara, and the Rumi Darwaza :)

3) Sports

Play cricket , The main sports hub is the K. D. Singh Babu Stadium, which also has a swimming pool and indoor games complex.

4) Residency

the Residency Complex, the British Residency is a complex of buildings that once served as the residence of the British Resident General.

5) Dilkusha Kothi

Located on the banks of the River Gomti, Dilkusha Kothi is the remains of a house that was built in the early 19th century. Built in the English baroque style of architecture

6) Clock Tower

Husainabad Clock Tower Located adjacent to the Rumi Darwaza, Husainabad Clock Tower is another heritage monument dotting the cityscape of Lucknow.
